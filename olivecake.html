Olive Oil Cake

For those who prefer a slightly spongier, finer texture.

The whipped egg makes for a soft and fine-crumbed texture!

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 1/4 tsp grated lemon zest
  • 3/4 cup extra virgin oilve oil
  • 3/4 cup milk

Directions

  1. Adjust oven rack to middle position and heat to 350 degrees
  2. Grease a 9-inch springform pan
  3. Whisk flour, baking powder and salt
  4. Using a mixer with the whisk attachment, whip eggs on medium speed until foamy, about 1 minute
  5. Add sugar and lemon zest, then increase speed to high. Whip until mixture is fluffy and pale yellow, about 3 minutes
  6. Reduce speed to medium and slowly pour in the olive oil, about 1 minute
  7. Add half of the flour mix on low speed for 1 minute before adding milk
  8. Add the remainder of the flour mix until just incorporated
  9. Transfer batter to prepared pan, then sprinkle 2 tsp sugar over the surface
  10. Bake 40-45 minutes, then let cool for 15 minutes. Remove the side of pan and let cake cool completely, about 1 1/2 hours
